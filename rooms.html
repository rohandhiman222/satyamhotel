About Us

Offering Wi-Fi connectivity at a surcharge to its patrons, Satyam Hotel is a budget accommodation in Tohana, situated 3 km away from Tohana Railway Station. RITZ Miniplex is a local theater located 2 km away from the premises. Guests are offered accommodation in well-kept spacious rooms and apartments. Some of the in-room amenities offered are bed, air-conditioner, bottled drinking water, refrigerator, separate seating area and attached bathroom. Satyam Hotel offers meal service such as breakfast and dinner on prior request. In addition to this, basic conveniences such as room service, pick-up/drop facility and parking space are also provided. Placed at a distance of 1 km from Tohana Bus Stand, this hotel in Tohana has ATM, hospital, supermarket and multiple eateries within a range of 1.5 km. Places of interest include Shagun Marriage Palace (3 km) and Guruduwara Dhamtan Sahib Novi Patshahi (14 km).
